# Coterie – Website Brief

## Brand
- **Name:** Coterie
- **Concept:** High-end, curated driving experiences across Europe for supercar/performance car owners
- **Clients bring their own cars; Coterie provides the experience, logistics, and social element**
- **Price range:** £10–15k per tour (NOT displayed on site)
- **Tone:** Premium, aspirational, understated. Luxury lifestyle, NOT car dealership.

## Target Audience
- Self-made, entrepreneurial individuals who own performance/supercars
- Aspirational, not ostentatious; premium but approachable

## Key Differentiator
- Social, curated driving experiences where every detail is managed
- Small, like-minded groups creating a shared, memorable journey

## Pages

### 1. Homepage
- Hero video/fullscreen visual: cars, scenic European routes, social group interaction
- Clear headline + 1–2 lines communicating premium experience
- CTA: "Enquire / Apply"
- Highlight USP: curated, social driving tours, seamless high-end experience

### 2. Tours / Experiences
- Tour overview: route, duration, hotels, dining, included services, support
- Emphasis on social experience: small, curated groups
- "Limited spaces" messaging
- Enquiry form CTA (no pricing)

### 3. About Us
Copy:
> At Coterie, we create curated driving experiences for those who want more than just a journey.
>
> At the heart of what we do is a passion for performance cars, exceptional routes, and unforgettable moments. We design bespoke European tours that bring together some of the world's most iconic roads, from alpine passes to coastal highways, paired with carefully selected hotels, dining, and experiences along the way.
>
> Our focus is simple: to deliver seamless, high-end driving experiences where every detail is taken care of. From route planning and logistics to on-the-ground support, everything is handled so you can focus on what matters most, the drive.
>
> Whether joining us for a single tour or becoming part of a wider community, each experience is designed to go beyond the expected, combining great driving with the kind of moments that make the journey truly memorable.

### 4. Enquiry / Booking Flow
- Capture: name, car, preferred tour, short text about what they want
- Option for CRM or email follow-up

### 5. Gallery / Media
- High-quality images/videos of routes, hotels, cars, social interactions

### 6. Membership Page (Future)
- Capture interest now even if launch is later

## Design & UX
- Mobile-first, fast-loading, clean, minimal, high-end feel
- Luxury lifestyle rather than "car dealership"
- Strong visuals, full-screen hero
- Consistent CTA placement for enquiries
- Simple, intuitive navigation

## Branding Notes
- Avoid referencing client demographics explicitly
- Avoid buzzwords like "new generation"
- Emphasize experience, curation, social element
